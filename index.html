Snack Time!!

Instructions

  • Move your animal left and right by pressing the left/right arrow keys
  • Protip: If you go past the screen you wrap around
  • Switch animals py pressing A (dog), S (cat), D (monkey), or F (rabbit)
  • Use the correct animal to eat their favorite food
  • Everytime food falls to the ground or you catch the wrong food with the wrong animal you lose a life
  • Once you lose three lives, it's game over!
