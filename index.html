Snack Time!!

Instructions: Move your animal left and right by pressing the left/right arrow keys

Protip: If you go past the screen you wrap around

Switch animals py pressing A (dog), S (cat), D (monkey), or F (rabbit)

Use the correct animal to eat their favorite food

Everytime food falls to the ground or you catch the wrong food with the wrong animal (everyone knows dogs don't like to eat bananas!) You lose a life (hearts on the top left corner). Once you lose three lives, it's game over!
